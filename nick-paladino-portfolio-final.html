04
Digital Experience
Process

I didn't start in tech — I studied cinematography and filmmaking at the New York Film Academy and earned a degree in Telecommunication & Film with a Business focus at the University of Alabama. Storytelling, light, and visual composition were my first language. That lens — understanding audience, narrative flow, and emotional impact — is what I bring to every digital experience I touch.

Discover

Business & context immersion. Identify stakeholder goals. Competitive research & trends analysis. Empathize with users' needs. Analyze data & behaviors.

Define

Define the experience framework. Map architecture, journeys & flows. Identify key personas. Workshop & brainstorm with teams. Prioritize features with stakeholders.

Design

Design the full experience. Build a design language and system. Provide annotated wireframes. Deliver specifications for engineering. Build interactive prototypes.

Iterate

Test the designed experience. Analyze user behavior. Optimize design outcomes. Handover to engineering team and QA the experience end-to-end.

Improve

Optimize the experience. Support launches, audit results. Identify improvements & recommend new feature launches. Iterate on the workflow and retrospect.
